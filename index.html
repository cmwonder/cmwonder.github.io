Top 3 Attractions in Zhubei

1. Hsinchu Zoo

Located in the heart of Zhubei, Hsinchu Zoo is home to over 100 species of animals.

2. Zhubei Sports Park

A great place for outdoor activities, featuring jogging tracks, bike paths, and play areas.

3. Hsinchu Science Park

This technology park is known for its innovative companies and beautiful surroundings.
